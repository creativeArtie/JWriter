Ways to edit for MainSpan
- add from nothing
- remove to nothing
- reparse span if:
    - normal text edited
- split when:
    - new heading is set: \n + "="
    - new note is set:
        - while as note: \n + "\!%" + [spaces] + "@"
        - while as section: \n + "\!%"
        - while as section: \n + "\!>"
    - new outline is set: \n + "!#
- merge when the following stop existing
    - while as section: \n + "="
    - while as section: \n + "!#"
    - while as note:  \n + "!%"
    - while as note:  \n + "
